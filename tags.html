Make a perfect cup of tea with just a few steps!

Ingredients

1 cup water
1 tea bag
1 Teaspoon sugar(optional)
1/2 cup milk(optional)
1 slice of lemon(optional)

Instructions

1.Boil water.
2.Place tea bag in a cup.
3.Pour hot water over the tea.
4.steep for3-5 minutes.
5.Add sugar on honey if you like.
6.Add milk if you prefer.
7.Add lemon if you desire.
8.Enjoy your Tea!
